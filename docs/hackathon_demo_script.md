## 🎭 Delivery Tips

### Pacing:
- **0:00-0:15:** Fast, energetic (hook them)
- **0:15-0:45:** Slow down, let them absorb the score
- **0:45-1:15:** Build tension, then **dramatic reveal** of adaptation
- **1:15-1:35:** Fast again, show polish
- **1:35-1:50:** Rapid-fire technical flex
- **1:50-2:00:** Slow, confident close

### Body Language:
- **Point** at critical gaps on screen
- **Pause** before "The entire roadmap rewrites itself"
- **Lean in** during technical flex
- **Step back** for the close

### Voice Modulation:
- **Louder:** "CRITICAL GAPS," "REWRITES ITSELF," "PRODUCTION-READY"
- **Softer:** "72% to hired... autonomously"
- **Faster:** Technical specs
- **Slower:** Market-Ready Score reveal

## 🎯 Anticipated Questions & Answers

**Q: "How does the AI know when to adapt the roadmap?"**
> "Great question. After every quiz, our simulation engine sends the user's score and weak topics back to the AI. If performance drops below 70%, Gemini 2.5 Flash re-runs the skill prioritization chain and injects remedial content. It's fully autonomous—no manual intervention."

**Q: "What makes this different from Coursera or Udemy?"**
> "Those platforms are content libraries. We're an **Agentic Career Architect**. They give you 10,000 courses. We give you the **exact 12 weeks** you need, adapted in real-time to your failures. They're static. We're autonomous."

**Q: "Is this actually deployed?"**
> "Yes. We have Redis caching live, Gemini API integrated, and a premium UI that looks like a $50/month SaaS. We're not pitching a concept—we're pitching a **market-ready product**."

**Q: "How do you monetize?"**
